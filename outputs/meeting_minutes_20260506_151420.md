## Summary
The Denver City Council meeting focused on the observance of Indigenous Peoples Day, including the reading and adoption of Proclamation 1127. The meeting included discussions about community events, particularly the upcoming Halloween parade, and emphasized the importance of recognizing Indigenous contributions to the city.

## Key Discussion Points
- Introduction of the meeting and Pledge of Allegiance.
- Approval of the minutes from the previous meeting on October 2nd.
- Announcement of the first Halloween parade in District 7 on October 21st.
- Presentation and reading of Proclamation 1127 in observance of Indigenous Peoples Day.
- Discussion on the significance of Indigenous Peoples Day and the contributions of Indigenous communities to Denver.

## Decisions
- Proclamation 1127, in observance of Indigenous Peoples Day, was adopted.

## Takeaways
- The Denver City Council officially recognized October 9, 2017, as Indigenous Peoples Day.
