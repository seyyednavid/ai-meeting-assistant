# Meeting Minutes

## Meeting Overview
- Date: October 9, 2017
- Location: Denver City Council Chambers
- Attendees: All council members present

## Summary
The meeting focused on approving the minutes from October 2nd and announcing a Halloween parade. The agenda also included reading and adopting Proclamation 1127, which officially observes Indigenous Peoples Day in the City and County of Denver.

## Key Discussion Points
- Councilman Lopez presented a proclamation regarding Indigenous Peoples Day.
- The proclamation highlighted the contributions of Indigenous peoples to Denver's history and culture.
- The confluence of Cherry Creek and South Platte River was mentioned as an inspiration for the logo design, which features water as its main symbol.

## Decisions
- Proclamation 1127 adopted by unanimous vote.

## Action Items
Not specified

## Takeaways
The meeting concluded with a proclamation officially recognizing Indigenous Peoples Day in Denver. The city will celebrate and honor the contributions of Indigenous peoples to their history, culture, and future.
